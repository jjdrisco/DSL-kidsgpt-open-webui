How do I clean my room in like 10 minutes so it looks clean enough and my mom stops coming in here? Tell me like a cheat code order.

Okayyy I got you 😎✨  
Here’s your **10-Minute “Looks Clean” Cheat Code Order** (speed run mode):

### ⏱️ Minute 1–2: TRASH ATTACK
- Grab a trash bag.
- Throw away all trash.  
Wrappers, papers, random junk = GONE.  
👉 This alone makes your room look 40% cleaner.

---

### ⏱️ Minute 3–4: CLOTHES SWEEP
- Dirty clothes → all in hamper (or one pile in closet if you’re desperate).
- Clean clothes → quick fold or stack on chair/dresser.
👉 No clothes on the floor = instant upgrade.

---

### ⏱️ Minute 5–6: BED HACK
- Make your bed fast.
You don’t need perfect corners.
Just:
1. Pull sheets straight.
2. Throw blanket on neatly.
3. Fluff pillow.

✅ A made bed makes the whole room look 60% cleaner even if other stuff isn’t perfect.

---

### ⏱️ Minute 7–8: SURFACE CLEAR
- Clear off desk and nightstand.
- Stack books neatly.
- Put random items in one basket or drawer.

If you don’t know where something goes?
👉 Put it in a “deal with later” box.

---

### ⏱️ Minute 9–10: FLOOR CHECK
- Quick floor scan.
- Kick anything random into its place.
- Do a fast straighten of anything obvious.

---

### 💡 Pro Level Move:
Spray a little air freshener or open a window.  
A room that smells clean = feels clean.

---

There you go. Speed-run complete 🏆
