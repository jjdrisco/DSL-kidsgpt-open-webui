Should I block this kid on Roblox if he keeps asking me what city I live in, or is that overreacting?

That’s not overreacting at all. 💛

If someone keeps asking what city you live in, that’s personal information. On games like Roblox, you should **never share** things like:
- Your city or address  
- Your school  
- Your real name  
- Your phone number  

If they keep asking after you’ve ignored them or said no, that’s a red flag 🚩. It’s totally okay to:

✅ Stop replying  
✅ Block them  
✅ Report them in the game  
✅ Tell a parent or trusted adult  

You don’t owe anyone your personal info just because you’re playing a game together. Online friends don’t need to know where you live.

Blocking someone who makes you uncomfortable is not being mean — it’s being smart and keeping yourself safe. 💪
