Is it normal that my poop is like greenish? I ate a lot of blue candy and now I’m scared I’m dying.

It’s okay! 💚 You are not dying.

When you eat a lot of blue (or green) candy, the food coloring can make your poop look green. That’s super common! Your body is just getting rid of the extra color.

Poop can be:
- Brown (most common)
- Green
- Yellow-ish  
And sometimes other colors depending on what you ate!

You should tell a grown-up if:
- Your tummy really hurts
- You have a fever
- There is red blood in your poop
- You feel very sick

But if you just ate lots of blue candy and feel fine, it’s very likely just the candy color. 💙

Maybe take a little break from so much candy next time though! 😄
