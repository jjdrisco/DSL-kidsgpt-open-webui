Should I stop being friends with her if she keeps like copying my stuff and then saying it was her idea?

That sounds really frustrating. It’s not fun when someone copies you and then says it was their idea. I can understand why that would hurt.

Before deciding to stop being friends, you could try a few things:

**1. Talk to her calmly.**  
You could say something like, “It hurts my feelings when you copy my idea and say it was yours.” Try to say it in a calm way, not during an argument.

**2. Notice how she reacts.**  
If she says sorry and tries to stop, that’s a good sign.  
If she laughs, ignores you, or keeps doing it, that’s important information too.

**3. Set small boundaries.**  
Maybe don’t share your ideas with her right away. See if giving a little space helps.

**4. Think about how you feel around her.**  
Do you usually feel happy and supported? Or annoyed and upset a lot? Good friendships should feel mostly good, even if there are small problems sometimes.

You don’t have to suddenly “break up” a friendship. You can slowly spend more time with other friends if this one isn’t treating you well.

If it keeps bothering you, it might also help to talk to a parent, teacher, or another trusted adult.
