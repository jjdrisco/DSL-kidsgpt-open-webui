Ooo fun!! 😄 Here’s a **sleepover checklist** that’s cool (not baby-ish) and actually helpful.

---

## 🎒 What to Pack

### 🛏️ Sleep Stuff
- Pajamas
- Change of clothes for the next day
- Underwear & socks
- Toothbrush & toothpaste
- Hairbrush / hair ties
- Deodorant
- Face wash (if you use it)
- Any meds you need

### 🛌 Extras (Optional but Smart)
- Sleeping bag or blanket
- Pillow (if you’re picky about pillows)
- Stuffed animal (no shame 😌)
- Phone + charger
- Headphones
- Flip flops (if sharing a bathroom)

---

## 🎲 Fun Games (Not Baby Stuff)

### 🎮 Chill + Fun
- **Would You Rather? (Extreme version)**  
- **Two Truths and a Lie**
- **Cards Against Humanity (Family/PG version if needed)**
- **Uno or Uno Flip**
- **Board games like Clue or Monopoly Deal**
- **Jackbox games (if someone has it)**
- **Mario Kart / Smash Bros / Just Dance**

### 👻 Nighttime Fun
- Flashlight tag (if allowed)
- Mild spooky stories (not nightmare-level 😅)
- Glow stick hide-and-seek
- Truth or Dare (keep it safe + kind!)

### 🎬 Cozy Option
- Movie marathon (vote on genre!)
- Make a ranking list of weird snacks or movies
- Do a mini “awards show” for funniest moments

---

## 🍿 Non-Messy Snack Ideas

(No sticky fingers. No orange dust explosions.)

### ✅ Good Picks
- Popcorn (lightly salted, not super buttery)
- Pretzel sticks or pretzel bites
- Goldfish crackers
- Chex Mix
- Mini muffins
- Granola bars
- Rice Krispie treats (not melted)
- Grapes (pre-washed)
- Apple slices (not too juicy)
- String cheese
- Yogurt tubes
- Mini sandwiches (like sliders)

### 🍫 Sweet but Safe
- Mini chocolate bars
- M&M’s (less melty than chocolate bars)
- Gummy candies
- Oreo thins
- Animal crackers (still elite 😌)

---

## 🎉 Bonus Idea
Make it themed:
- “Cozy Winter Vibes”
- “Retro 2000s Night”
- “Game Tournament Night”
- “Spa + Chill Night” (face masks + movies)
