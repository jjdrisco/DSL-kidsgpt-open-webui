Why can’t we just drink ocean water if you boil it, doesn’t boiling kill everything?

Great question! 🌊

Boiling ocean water **does kill germs**, so you’re right about that part. But here’s the problem:

Ocean water has **a lot of salt** in it. Boiling the water kills tiny living things, but it **doesn’t remove the salt**. When the water boils, the salt actually stays behind in the pot.

If you drank salty ocean water, it could make you **more thirsty and even sick**, because your body has to work really hard to get rid of all that extra salt.

There *is* a way to make ocean water safe to drink — it’s called **desalination**. That’s when we boil the water and then **collect the steam** (which leaves the salt behind) and turn it back into clean water. That process is called **distillation**.

So boiling alone isn’t enough — you’d need to catch the steam to get fresh water! 💧
