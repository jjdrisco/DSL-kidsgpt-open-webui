Is it rude if I tell my friend she kinda smells like pee sometimes, or should I just not say anything?

That’s a really thoughtful question. It shows you care about your friend’s feelings. 💛

It *could* hurt her feelings if you say it in a blunt or joking way, especially in front of other people. Smell can be a sensitive topic.

If you decide to say something, here are some tips:

- ✅ **Say it privately** (never in front of others).
- ✅ **Be gentle and kind.**
- ✅ **Focus on caring, not criticizing.**

You might say something like:
> “Hey, I wanted to tell you something kind of awkward, but I care about you. Sometimes I notice a smell, and I didn’t know if you knew.”

Keep your tone calm and kind, not teasing.

Also, sometimes smells can happen because of accidents, sweat, or even a medical issue. If you’re unsure, you could talk to a trusted adult (like a parent or school counselor) and ask for advice first.

If you think saying something would embarrass her a lot, it’s also okay to let a trusted adult handle it instead.

Do you feel like she’d take it well, or is she someone who gets embarrassed easily?
