Okay wait—how do I make my phone stop showing message previews on the lock screen? Like step by step, I can’t find it, it’s embarrassing.

Totally get that 😭 lock screen previews can be *way* too revealing. I got you. Here’s how to turn them off depending on your phone:

---

## 📱 If you have an **iPhone**:

### Option 1: Hide message previews completely
1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **Notifications**
3. Tap **Show Previews**
4. Select **Never**  
   (or **When Unlocked** if you still want to see them when you’re using your phone)

That stops messages from showing the actual text on the lock screen.

---

### Option 2: Turn off previews just for Messages
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Notifications**
3. Scroll down and tap **Messages**
4. Tap **Show Previews**
5. Choose **Never** or **When Unlocked**

You can do this for apps like Instagram, Snapchat, etc. too.

---

## 🤖 If you have an **Android**:

It varies a bit by brand, but generally:

1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **Notifications**
3. Tap **Lock screen**
4. Look for something like:
   - **Notifications on lock screen**
   - **Hide content**
   - **Sensitive notifications**
5. Choose **Hide content** (or similar wording)
