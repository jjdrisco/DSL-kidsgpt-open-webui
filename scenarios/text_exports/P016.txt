Tell me if sharks can smell blood from like a mile away or is that fake. My cousin says it’s true and I think he’s lying.

That’s a great question! 🦈

Your cousin isn’t totally wrong… but it’s a little exaggerated.

Sharks have an **amazing sense of smell**. They can smell tiny amounts of blood in the water — even just a few drops in a big swimming pool! 😲

But can they smell it from a mile away?  
Not exactly like in the movies. It depends on things like:
- How much blood there is  
- How the water is moving  
- Which kind of shark it is  

Usually, it’s more like **hundreds of meters away**, not miles and miles.

So your cousin isn’t completely lying… but he might be making it sound more dramatic than it really is. 😄
