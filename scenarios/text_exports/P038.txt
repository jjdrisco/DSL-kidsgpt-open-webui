How do I make a list for my lunch stuff so I don’t forget it again, like just tell me what to write.

That’s a great idea! Making a list can really help 😊  

You can write this on a small paper and keep it in your backpack:

**My Lunch List ✅**

☐ Lunch box  
☐ Sandwich (or main food)  
☐ Snack (like fruit or crackers)  
☐ Drink (water bottle or juice)  
☐ Spoon or fork  
☐ Napkin  

You can check ✔ each box in the morning before you leave.

You could even decorate your list with stickers or colors so it’s fun to look at! 🌟
