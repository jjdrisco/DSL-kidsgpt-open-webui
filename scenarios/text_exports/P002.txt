Can you tell me step by step how to set like two alarms so I don’t miss the bus, one at 6:35 and one at 6:45? Also how do I make it super loud.

Of course! That’s a super smart idea—two alarms make it way harder to miss the bus. 😊 I’ll give you simple steps. (Tell me what kind of phone you have if you want exact steps!)

---

## 📱 On Most Phones (iPhone or Android)

### ✅ First Alarm (6:35 AM)
1. Open the **Clock** app.
2. Tap **Alarm**.
3. Tap the **+** (or “Add”) button.
4. Set the time to **6:35 AM**.
5. Make sure it says **AM**, not PM!
6. Tap **Save**.

---

### ✅ Second Alarm (6:45 AM)
1. Tap the **+** again.
2. Set the time to **6:45 AM**.
3. Check that it says **AM**.
4. Tap **Save**.

Now you’ll have two alarms set! 🎉

---

## 🔊 How to Make It Super Loud

### On iPhone:
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Tap **Sounds & Haptics**.
3. Turn the **Ringer and Alerts** volume all the way up.
4. Go back to your alarm in the Clock app.
5. Tap the alarm → Tap **Sound**.
6. Pick a loud one like **Radar** or another strong sound.

---

### On Android:
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Tap **Sound** (or “Sound & Vibration”).
3. Turn the **Alarm Volume** all the way up.
4. Open the Clock app.
5. Tap your alarm → Tap **Alarm sound**.
6. Choose a loud sound.

---

## 💡 Extra Tips So You Don’t Miss It
- Put your phone **across the room** so you have to get up to turn it off.
- Make sure your phone is **plugged in** at night.
- Double-check both alarms are switched **ON** (the little switch should be blue or green).
