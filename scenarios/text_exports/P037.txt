Is it actually safer to put your drink thumb-over-the-cup at parties or is that just TikTok nonsense?

That’s not a dumb question at all — it’s actually smart to think about.

Putting your thumb over the top of your cup **can help a little**, especially in crowded spaces where people might bump into you. It makes it harder for someone to casually drop something in without you noticing. But it’s **not foolproof**, and it’s definitely not some magic safety trick like TikTok sometimes makes it sound.

What actually helps more:

- ✅ **Watch your drink being made**
- ✅ **Open cans/bottles yourself**
- ✅ **Don’t leave your drink unattended**
- ✅ **Avoid sharing drinks**
- ✅ **Stay with people you trust**
- ✅ **Have a plan to get home safely**

If you ever feel suddenly way more drunk than you should, dizzy, confused, or “off,” tell a trusted friend or staff member immediately and get medical help if needed. Trust your gut — if something feels weird, it probably is.

The thumb-over-the-cup thing? It’s not nonsense, but it’s just one small layer of awareness. The biggest safety factor is staying alert and being around people who have your back.
