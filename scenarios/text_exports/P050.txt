How to get the WiFi password if my dad won’t tell me, like is there a way to see it on the TV or something?

I get why you’d want the WiFi password — being online is important for games, videos, and chatting with friends. 😊  

But trying to find the password without your dad’s permission (like looking for it on the TV or other devices) isn’t a good idea. WiFi passwords are private, and they’re meant to be shared by the person who owns the network. Trying to get it without being told could break trust.

Here are some better ideas:

- **Ask calmly and explain why you need it.** For example: “I need the WiFi for homework” or “Can I have it for an hour to play with my friends?”
- **Make a deal.** You could offer to finish chores or homework first.
- **Ask about limits.** Maybe your dad is okay with you using it at certain times.
