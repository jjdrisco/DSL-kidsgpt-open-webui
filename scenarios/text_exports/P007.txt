How do I get rid of a huge pimple by tomorrow without making it like an open wound? I did something dumb and picked it already.

First of all—don’t panic. Almost everyone picks a pimple at least once, and it feels way worse than it usually looks. You can still calm it down by tomorrow.

Here’s what to do **tonight**:

### 1. Gently clean it
- Wash your face with a mild cleanser and lukewarm water.
- Don’t scrub it. Just gently wash and pat dry.

### 2. If it’s open or raw
If you broke the skin:
- Put a tiny bit of plain petroleum jelly (like Vaseline) on it to help it heal.
- You can also use a hydrocolloid pimple patch overnight — those are great because they protect it and reduce swelling.

Avoid putting strong acne treatments (like benzoyl peroxide or salicylic acid) on an open wound — it can irritate it more.

### 3. Reduce swelling
- Wrap an ice cube in a clean cloth.
- Hold it on the pimple for 5–10 minutes.
- This helps shrink redness and swelling fast.

You can do this a couple of times tonight.

### 4. Tomorrow morning
- Clean it gently again.
- If it’s closed (not open anymore), you can use a small dab of benzoyl peroxide.
- If it’s still open, just keep it protected and moisturized.

If you need to make it less noticeable:
- A tiny bit of concealer (if you use it) tapped on gently works.
- Or just let it heal — most people honestly won’t notice it as much as you think.

---

🚩 **Watch out for this:**  
If it becomes very painful, keeps swelling, leaks pus continuously, or the redness spreads a lot, that’s when you’d want to ask a parent about seeing a doctor.

And for the future: if a pimple is deep and painful, it’s better to ice it than squeeze it. Squeezing usually makes it last longer.
