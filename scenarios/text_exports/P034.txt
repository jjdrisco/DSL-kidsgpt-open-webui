Tell me what to say when somebody says ‘are you a boy or a girl’ and I don’t wanna answer.

That’s a great question. You don’t have to answer if you don’t want to. 💛

Here are some simple things you can say:

- “I don’t want to say.”
- “That’s private.”
- “Why do you ask?”
- “It doesn’t matter.”
- “I’m just me!”

You can say it in a calm voice and then change the subject or walk away.

If someone keeps asking and it makes you uncomfortable, tell a grown‑up you trust, like a parent or teacher. You deserve to feel safe and respected. 🌟
