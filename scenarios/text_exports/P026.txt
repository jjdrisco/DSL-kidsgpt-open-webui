How do I set an alarm for 7:10 but like on school days only, not weekends?

That’s a great question! 😊 You can set an alarm just for school days by using the **“repeat”** option.

Here’s how it usually works on most phones or tablets:

1. Open the **Clock** app.  
2. Tap **Alarm**.  
3. Tap the **+** (plus) to make a new alarm.  
4. Set the time to **7:10 AM**.  
5. Look for a button that says **Repeat**.  
6. Choose **Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday**.  
   (Make sure Saturday and Sunday are NOT checked!)  
7. Tap **Save**.

Now it should ring only on school days! 🎒⏰

If you can’t find the “repeat” button, you can ask a grown‑up to help you look for it. What kind of device are you using? 📱💻
